Origins

Reyzed Gaming is a site developed to allow gamers to continue playing games while watching some of their favorite streamers on sites like Beam and Twitch The idea was originally to turn the rng based currency events that a lot of streamers have, wheter they call them dungeons, heists, or whatever into an actual playable game that players could either work together in or compete with one another in to earn channel currency while watching the stream. The idea came to me the first time I was watching the one and only Penny Stone (she's awesome, check her out here) and she explained what her "!dungeon" command did and I confused it with being an actual game that started playing and since then I have wanted to make something like this.

The Future

Currently there are plans to add a database backend to the game and website in general to both add a way to keep track of one's score over time and to allow for populating the front page with currently live streamers instead of it being a static list. Also a bot is in the works to give streamers more control over the games in their channel and allow them to add stream integration for things like starting a game with commands and using the currency for things in channel. This bot will be entirely stand alone, but I will be trying to integrate with already existing bots that perform similar functions for ease of use. If you have any suggestions feel free to send them to me via the contact section of the site.
